Why go vegan?

Animals

Going vegan is one of the best things you can do to help stop animal cruelty. By refusing to pay for animal products, you reduce the demand for them, which ensures fewer animals are bred to suffer and die on farms and in slaughterhouses


Land of Hope and Glory

Health

By skipping animal products you remove cholesterol from your diet and dramatically lower your staturated fat. All of this reduces risk of certain health conditions, including ischemic heart disease, type 2 diabetes, hypertension, certain types of cancer, and obesity


Cowspiracy

Enviroment

The animal agriculture industry is one of the biggest polluters in the world. It is also the biggest contriubter to deforestation and species loss. A vegan diet needs far less land to produce and therefore dramatically decreases your carbon footprint
